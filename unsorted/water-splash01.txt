Surround recording of somebody jumping of a stone jetty into the basin of a small fishing harbor, from a height of about 3m. It is a warm summer evening, and some crickets can be heard above the waves gently lapping on the jetty. The jump and corresponding splash take place in the middle of the recording, the recorder is situated on the jetty next to the jump-spot, facing the harbor basin.
Recorded with a Zoom H2.
